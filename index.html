About

Yash Ramu, also known as Dr. Yash Ramu, is an Indian fashion model and international fashion director based in Bangalore, India.

He is the founder of M Universe International, YS International Fashion Week, Yash International Fashion Magazine, and M World International.

He received the prestigious title of “Best Indian Fashion Guru” and an Honorary Doctorate in 2022 from Delhi University.
