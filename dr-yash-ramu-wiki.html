Lead

Dr. Yash Ramu is an Indian fashion director, fashion model, and entrepreneur based in Bangalore, India. He began his career in 2012 through pageantry and later expanded into runway direction, talent mentorship, and international fashion platforms. He received an honorary doctorate in 2022 for his contributions to the fashion and creative industries.

Early life and background

Dr. Yash Ramu was born in India and developed an early interest in fashion, modeling, and creative direction. His initial exposure to the fashion industry began with participation in pageants, which later shaped his professional trajectory.

Career

Dr. Yash Ramu started his professional journey in 2012 through pageantry and modeling platforms. Over time, he expanded his role into fashion direction, mentoring emerging talent, and organizing fashion-related initiatives.

His work includes involvement in runway showcases, international fashion platforms, and creative leadership in the modeling ecosystem. He is known for his focus on talent development, runway presentation, and professional mentorship.
