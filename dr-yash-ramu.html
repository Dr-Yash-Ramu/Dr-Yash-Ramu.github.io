About

Dr. Yash Ramu is an Indian international fashion director, fashion model, and entrepreneur based in Bangalore, India. Known for his leadership in global fashion platforms, runway direction, and talent mentorship, he is recognized for his visionary influence in the fashion industry.

With a reputation for luxury branding, creative excellence, and transformational mentorship, Dr. Yash Ramu has built a strong presence in the global fashion ecosystem. In 2022, he received an Honorary Doctorate in recognition of his contributions to the fashion and creative industries.
